Pizza Recipe

This is a Margerita Pizza. It is made of dry yeast for the crust, a floured surface, a tomato sauce base, and with a layer of Mozzarella Cheese, and a basil leaf to garnish on top.

Ingredients:
  • Dry Yeast
  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • basil leaves (optional)
Steps:
  1. Get a Dry yeast and spread it out in the shape of a circle with your hands.
  2. Lay on a flour surface.
  3. Put in a pan.
  4. Put the Tomato Sauce on top.
  5. Add the Mozzarella Cheese.
  6. Put the pan on the stovetop and leave it on medium heat for 10 minutes.
  7. Lay it on a plate.
  8. This is optional, but you can put two basil leaves in the center of the pie.
  9. Cut into eight pieces and enjoy!
