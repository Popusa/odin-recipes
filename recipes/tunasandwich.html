Tuna Sandwich Recipe

This is a Tuna Sandwich. it is made using only three ingredients and it has the shape of a triangle. A great lunch for your kids!

Ingredients:
  • Two loaves of White Toast
  • A can of Tuna Chunks
  • Mayonnaise
Steps:
  1. Lay the tuna chunks on an empty plate.
  2. Mash the chunks until the tuna is all very small pieces.
  3. Get two loaves of white toast and put them in the microwave for 20 seconds.
  4. Take the toast out of the microwave and cut them into the shape of a triangle.
  5. Put the tuna on one side of a toast.
  6. Lay a teaspoon of mayonnaise on one side of the other toast.
  7. Put the two pieces of bread together and enjoy your meal!
