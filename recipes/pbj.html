Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

This is a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich. it is made the same way as our Tuna Sandwich recipe but with different ingredients. It is very tasty you should try it!

Ingredients:
  • Two loaves of White Toast
  • Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jam
Steps:
  1. Get two loaves of white toast and put them in the microwave for 20 seconds.
  2. Take the toast out of the microwave and cut them into the shape of a triangle.
  3. Lay a tablespoon of peanut butter on the side of a toast.
  4. Lay a tablespoon of strawberry jam on the side of the other toast.
  5. Put the two pieces of bread together and enjoy your meal!
